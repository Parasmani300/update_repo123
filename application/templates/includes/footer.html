About the Organisation


Cosmo Juris is a student-driven online platform for Law students, researchers, scholars and enthusiasts which seeks to bring Legal Fraternity together. We acknowledge, and encourage the legal fraternity to come up with their research articles in the form of blogs and thereby provide them with a one-stop platform to express their Knowledge. We have a full-fledged team of writers and Editors who brings to you Information related to various legal developments, and opportunities for law students. We believe in providing Law students and researchers with our exclusive contents and opportunities which will keep them Updated with the legal arena.
